About Me

Hello!!!

The name's Tori, or Byte (which-ever goes), and I'm the webmaster of this (INTER).face. I'm a black American artist who resides somewhere in southwestern USA, producing visual works within the confines of my bedroom. My inspirations and motivations include the neo-y2k movement, nerdcore techno, pre-2010s nostalgia, and the like. There are many other things I want to list off, but I don't wanna bore you, so onto my webmastering experience!


I was first invested in web design around 2018, back when Carrd was (and still somewhat is) the norm for casuals. The reason why I chose to commit to HTML/CSS web design as of July 2024 was for various reasons, ranging from the freemium nature of drag-and-drop web builders, the staleness of today's cyberspace, and just so I can seem more productive and find a different hobby that isn't drawing.


Aside from drawing and coding, I enjoy maximalism, collecting and learning about lost and obscure media, electronic music, and reading wikis!
