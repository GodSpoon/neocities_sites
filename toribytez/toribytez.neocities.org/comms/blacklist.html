Blacklist Policy

What happens if I'm blacklisted?

Punishments may vary depending on the situation.

  • 1-month blacklists include:
    • failing to submit a payment within 3 days
    • failing to respond to any of my DMs
  • When you get temporarily blacklisted, you can still donate. You just cannot commission me until the duration of your blacklist expires.
  • After the expiration, you will be on a leniency; repeated offenses will result in harsher punishments.
  • Misusing your commission, will get you permanently blacklisted, meaning a hardblock on all of my socials.
    Examples are:
    • removing my watermark
    • profiting off of my work
    • prank/troll commissions
  • If your name is on the blacklist as a preemptive measure, then you're permanently not allowed to interact. It means:
    • You've made me feel extremely uncomfortable.
    • You have a negative reputation in a certain community I'm a part of.
