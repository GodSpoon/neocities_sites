About me

Hello! My name's Byte (or Tori, which-ever goes), and I'm a black American artist who's heavily inspired by the neo-y2k and frutiger movement of the 2020s and visionary art! My works combine all sorts of internet culture and fandom with 90s-2000s nostalgia, Japanese influences, mysticism, and futurism. I'm also autistic.

I was first invested in web design around 2018 thanks to Carrd (the site I used as building blocks until July 2024). However, I wasn't interested in HTML-CSS coding because some aspects feel overwhelming and complicated, so I tend to collect graphics for future purposes from various sites, from the reblogs of Tumblr to the rabbit holes of Neocities. But my perspective changed when I was introduced to sheezy.art from Twitter back in March 2024. I know it's a successor to the original SheezyArt.com, as well as a great alternative to the ever-infamous DeviantArt. Currently, I'm still teaching myself how to code, but I'm slowly improving and learning.

Aside from web design and drawing, I enjoy graphic design, collecting and listening to music, learning about obscure things such as lost media, and reading wikis!
